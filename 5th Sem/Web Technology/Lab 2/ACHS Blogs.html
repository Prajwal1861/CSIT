Sports Week

Sports Week is a highly anticipated annual event that brings together students, athletes, and sports enthusiasts to celebrate teamwork, discipline, and physical excellence. The week is filled with competitive matches, friendly games, and energetic participation across various sports disciplines.

From traditional sports such as football, basketball, and volleyball to indoor games and athletic challenges, Sports Week offers opportunities for everyone to get involved. The event not only focuses on competition but also encourages leadership, cooperation, and a healthy lifestyle.

Beyond the games, Sports Week creates a vibrant atmosphere with cheering crowds, shared experiences, and lasting memories. It strengthens bonds within the college community and reminds everyone of the importance of balancing academics with physical well-being.
